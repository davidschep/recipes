# Sekerpare (Cookies)
Birthday or bayram cookies.

## Ingredients
* 250g sugar
* 1/4tsp lemon juice
* 380g flour
* 125g butter
* 80g sugar
* 1 egg
* 1tbsp baking powder
* 18 unsalted pistache

## Steps
1. Boil 250g of sugar with 450ml of water, add lemon juice, boil for 15 minutes, cool down
2. Preheat oven at 180c
3. Mix 125g of cubed butter, 80g sugar, egg, and baking powder and knead to dough, keep adding in flour
4. Roll 18 balls, add a pistache on top
5. Cook in oven for 20/25 minutes
6. Pour over cooled down sirup immediately, cover, rest for 3 hours, serve
