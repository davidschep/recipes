# Schug (Koriander and Green Pepper Sauce)
Schug is a vibrant, fiery hot sauce originating from Yemeni cuisine.

## Ingredients
* Koriander
* Parsley
* Cardamom
* Green peppers
* Raw garlic cloves
* Cumin
* Olive oil
* Fresh ginger (optional)

## Steps
1. Roughly chop ingredients and blend together in food processor
2. Serve
