# Yanyana Tavası (Roasted Lamb with Veggies)

## Ingredients 
Meat and marinade
* 1kg boneless lamb
* 1 tbsp each of Tomato and pepper paste
* 2 tbsp olive oil
* 100 ml kefir or yogurt
* 2 tsp cumin
* 2 tsp chili powser
* 2 tsp oregano or thyme
* 2 tsp salt

Veggies
* 1 eggplant
* 3 green turkish peppers
* 2 long red peppers
* 4 medium potatoes
* 3 medium tomatoes
* 10 garlic cloves
* oil

## Steps
1. Slice lamb into 1 or 2 cm cubes
2. Mix all marinade ingredients and leave for an hour up to a day
3. Chop veggies into 2 cm pieces and toss them in salt and some oil
4. Spread everything in a baking tray and roast for 1+ hour covered at 180c
5. Take off cover and roast for 10 more minutes uncovered at 220c

## Variations
* Serve with beğendi on the side (bechamel with roasted eggplant and cheese)
