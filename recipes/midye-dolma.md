# Midye Dolma (Filled Mussels)
Mussels filled with rice

## Ingredients
* Raw shelled mussels
* Turkish rice
* White onion, finely chopped
* Tomato, grated
* Black pepper
* Cinnamon
* Dried mint
* Parsley
* Tomato paste
* Olive oil
* Hot water
* Salt
* Lemon wedges to serve

## Steps
1. Cook onion in olive oil, add tomato paste, spices, and herbs
2. Add washed rice and boiled water, cook until al dente
3. Clean mussels very well
4. Slice open mussels with knife
5. Fill mussels with rice and close
6. Put all mussels in a pot, add some water, and cook for 15 minutes
7. Serve hot or cold
