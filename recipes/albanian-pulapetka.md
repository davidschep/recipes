# Albanian Pula (chicken with yufka)
Yufka dough with chicken and walnuts.

## Ingredients
* Whole chicken or chicken legs
* Walnuts
* Olive oil
* Yufka

## Steps
1. Cook the chicken and reserve the broth
2. Toast the walnuts in olive oil
3. Toast the yufka
4. Dress the plate with yufka
5. Layer over the chicken, walnuts, and pour over chicken broth
