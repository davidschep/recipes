# Çiğ Köfte
Bulgur meatballs

## Ingredients
* Fine bulgur (2 cups)
* Boiling water (2 cups)
* Tomato paste (1 tbsp)
* Pepper paste (1 tbsp)
* Olive oil (80ml)
* Parsley chopped
* White onion (1 medium)
* Chili flakes (3 tbsp)
* Isot (3 tbsp)
* Pomegranate molasses (2 tbsp)
* Chili powder (optional)
* Salt (1+ tsp)
* Lemon juice (1 lemon)

## Variations
* lentil kofte
* Çiğ köfte with meat

## Serve with
* lavaş
* iceberg lettuce
* lemon
* nar ekşisi
* pickles
