# Çiğ Köfte
(bulgur meatballs)

## Ingredients:
* Fine bulgur (2 cups)
* Boiling water (2 cups)
* Tomato paste (1 tbsp)
* Pepper paste (1 tbsp)
* Olive oil (80ml)
* Parsley chopped
* White onion (1 medium)
* Chili flakes (3 tbsp)
* Isot (3 tbsp)
* Pomegranate molasses (2 tbsp)
* Chili powder (optional)
* Salt (1+ tsp)
* Lemon juice (1 lemon)

## Serve with:
* lavaş
* iceberg lettuce
* lemon
* nar ekşisi
* pickles

## Alternatives:
1. lentil kofte
2. Çiğ köfte with meat
