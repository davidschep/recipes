# Çiğ Köfte (spiced bulgur meatballs)

## Ingredients
* Fine bulgur (2 cups)
* Boiling water (2 cups)
* Tomato paste (1 tbsp)
* Pepper paste (1 tbsp)
* Olive oil (80ml)
* Parsley chopped
* White onion (1 medium)
* Chili flakes (3 tbsp)
* Isot (3 tbsp)
* Pomegranate molasses (2 tbsp)
* Chili powder (optional)
* Salt (1+ tsp)
* Lemon juice (1 lemon)

## Steps
1. Add boiling water to bulgur
2. Use foodprocessor or knead bulgur
3. Mix in all ingredients

## Serve with
* Lavaş / tortillas
* Iceberg lettuce or arugola
* Lemon
* Nar ekşisi (pomegranate molasses)
* Pickles or spicy peppers

## Variations
* Lentil kofte
* Çiğ köfte with meat
