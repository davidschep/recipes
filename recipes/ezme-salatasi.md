# Ezme (grated tomato)
Grated tomato salad

## Ingredients
* 2 tomatoes
* 2 green peppers
* 1 onion
* 2 cloves of garlic
* Parsley
* Lemon juice
* Nar ekşisi (pomegranate molasses)
* 1 tsp each of sumak, chili flakes, isot, salt, dried mint
* 50 ml olive oil

## Steps
1. Blend all ingredients in a food processor
