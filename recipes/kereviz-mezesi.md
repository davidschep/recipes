# Kereviz Mezesi (celeriac with yogurt)

## Ingredients
* Walnuts
* Celeriac
* Garlic
* Tahini
* Yogurt
* Garnish: pistachios, parsley, red pepper flakes, olive oil

## Variations
* Add grated green apple

## Steps
1. Grate celeriac and cook in olive oil
2. Mix with yogurt and tahini
3. Serve with garnish
