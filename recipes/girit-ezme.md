# Girit ezme
Green cheese dish

## Ingredients
* White cheese (tulum / ezine / feta)
* Pistachios (and/or walnuts)
* 1 clove of garlic
* Olive oil
* Cayenne
* Basil
* Red chili flakes

## Steps
1. Mix basil and walnuts in food processor
2. Add other ingredients
3. Optionally top with red chili flakes
