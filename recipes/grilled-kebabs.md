# Grilled kebabs
Different kinds of kebab for the bbq.

## Adana kebab
Ingredients:
* Ground beef/lamb
* Tomato/pepper paste
* Grated onion
* Grated/diced green/red pepper
* Spices and salt

Steps:
1. Knead mixture very well
2. Fold around flat skewer
3. Grill on charcoal

## Çiğer şiş
Ingredients:
* Beef/lamb liver
* Beef/lamb fat
* Salt, cumin, red pepper flakes
* Olive oil

Steps:
1. Marinate meat in olive oil, salt, spices
2. Alternate between meat and fat pieces
3. Grill on charcoal

## Kalp şiş (veal heart)
Ingredients:
* Beef/lamb heart
* Beef/lamb fat
* Salt, cumin, red pepper flakes
* Olive oil

Steps:
1. Marinate meat in olive oil, salt, spices
2. Alternate between meat and fat pieces
3. Grill on charcoal

## Marinated chicken thigh şiş
Ingredients:
* Chicken thigh
* Biber salça (red pepper paste)
* Chili flakes, cumin, salt

## Serve with
* Cold meze
* Bread
* Grilled tomatoes and peppers
* Whole spices on the table
