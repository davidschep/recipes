# Grilled kebabs
Different kinds of kebab for the bbq.

## Adana kebab
Ingredients:
* ground beef/lamb
* tomato/pepper paste
* onion
* pepper
* spices

## Çiğer şiş
Ingredients:
* beef liver
* fat

## Kalp şiş
Ingredients:
* beef/lamb heart
* fat

## Serve with
* cold meze
* bread
* grilled tomatoes and peppers
* whole spices on the table
