# Yoğurtlu biber ve patlıcan (yogurt with peppers and eggplant)

## Ingredients:
* 2 eggplants
* 3 green turkish peppers
* 2 long red peppers
* Oil
* Few tbsp Turkish yogurt
* Chopped parsley
* Red pepper flakes
* Shoestring fries for garnish

## Steps
1. Chop eggplant and peppers into 2cm pieces
2. Toss with oil and salt and roast in oven until browned
3. Cool down and mix with other ingredients
4. Right before serving top with shoestring fries as garnish
