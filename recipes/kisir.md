# Kısır
Bulgur salad

## Ingredients
* 2 cups fine bulgur
* 1.5 cups boiling water
* 2 table spoons tomato and/or pepper paste
* 80 ml olive oil
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp chili flakes
* Juice of 2 lemons
* 40 ml pomegranate molasses
* 6 green onions
* Parsley
* Fresh mint

## Steps
1. Combine the boiling water and bulgur
2. Let sit and mix well
3. Add olive oil and tomato/pepper paste
4. Add other ingredients and mix well
5. Serve
