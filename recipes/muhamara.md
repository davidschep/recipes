# Muhamarra
Tomato paste with walnuts

## Ingredients
* Walnuts
* Breadcrumbs
* Pomegranate molasses
* Garlic
* Pepper paste
* Tomato paste
* Cumin
* Lemon juice

## Steps
1. Toast walnuts and breadcrumbs in pan
2. Add garlic and pastes
3. Combine with other ingredients and serve
