# Pastrami
Homemade pastrami recipe.

## Ingredients (Per 1.4 kg Beef chuck):
- 35g nitrietzout
- 7g salt
- 2 tsp (6g) ground Black Peppercorns
- tsp (2.5g) ground coriander
- 21g brown sugar
- 1.2L water
- 0.5tsp ground ginger
- 1 black 1 green cardamom
- 2 cloves

## Steps 
1. Make corned beef by brining meat for 5 days
2. Desalinate by soaking in clean water for 2 hours, changing the water every 30 minutes
3. Pat dry and add the rub
4. Let sit for an hour at room temp
5. Cook for 2 hours at 250F uncovered
6. Cook covered for ~3.5 hours more until 200F inside (sometimes get stuck at 170F for a while)
7. Optionally cook at 450F uncovered for 10 minutes to get the crust to stick
8. Cool and then slice

## Rub (enough for 5lbs of brisket):
- 3 tbsp Black Peppercorns
- 2 tsp Pink Peppercorns
- 1/2 tsp White Pepper
- 1 tbsp whole coriander
- 1 tbsp mustard powder
- smoked paprika
