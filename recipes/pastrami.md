# Pastrami or Boiled Beef
Homemade pastrami recipe.

## Ingredients (Per 1.5 kg Beef chuck/brisket):
- 75g nitrietzout
- 2 tsp black Peppercorns
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 50g brown sugar
- 0.75L water
- 0.5 tsp ground ginger
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 1 bay leaf (crushed)
- 1 star anise (crushed)

## Steps 
1. Bring all brine ingredients to a boil and then let cood down
2. Make corned beef by brining meat for 5 days
3. After 5 days, wash off brine and salt
4. Boil in pressure cooker at high pressure for 40 minutes
5. Cool down and slice
