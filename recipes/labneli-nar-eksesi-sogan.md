# Labneli Nar Ekşili Soğan (onion with labneh)
Cooked onions with labneh.

## Ingredients
* 2 white onions
* Olive oil
* Salt
* Walnuts
* Pomegranate molasses
* Labneh (or cream cheese)
* Optional: top with pomegranate seeds, pistachios, and olive oil

## Steps
1. Cook big chunks of white onion with olive oil
2. Add walnuts
3. Add nar ekşisi 
4. Let cool
5. Mix with labneh
6. Optionally garnish
