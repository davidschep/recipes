# Arnavut ciğeri (fried liver)
Fried liver

## Ingredients
* 1 kg Liver
* Flour mixed with salt
* Neutral oil for frying
* Cumin
* Chili flakes

## Steps
1. Chop liver in pieces of 1 or 2 cm
2. Toss pieces in flour mix
3. Deep fry liver
4. Serve and toss cumin and chili flakes on top

## Notes:
* Serve with raw onion salad
