# Iraqi Amba (mango sauce)
Amba is a tangy, spiced mango condiment popular in Middle Eastern cuisine—especially in Israel and Iraq, made from pickled green mangoes, vinegar, salt, and spices like fenugreek, turmeric, and chili.

## Ingredients
* One green mango
* Ground fenugreek seeds 2 tablespoons (or go a little less)
* Hot paprika 1 tablespoon 
* Ground cumin 1 tablespoon 
* Salt 1.5 -2 tablespoons (USE LESS)
* Turmeric 2-3 tablespoons 
* juice of one lemon 
* Plain white vinegar 1 cup (about 250 ml) 
* Mustard 2 tablespoons 
* A third of a cup of olive oil¥

## Steps
1. Soak the ground fenugreek in water
2. Peel and slice the mango
3. Boil the mango until completely soft in the vinegar
4. Lightly toast the spices in olive oil and then add to the pot
5. Blend completely, sauce is ready to bottle up (possibly dilute with some water for desired thickness)
