# Roasted fennel
## Ingredients
* Fennel
* Raki/arak, 
* Tahini
* Lemon juice
* Orange
* Oregano
* Olive oil
* Sumak

## Steps
1. Slice the fennel in half, toss with olive oil and rakı/arak, roast in oven until caramelized
2. Slice orange, toss with olive oil, fennel, and oregano, and roast in oven until caramelized
3. Make tahini sauce by combining with lemon juice, water, salt
4. Dress plate with ingredients and sprinkle sumak
