# Atom (yogurt with chili oil)
Yogurt with chili butter

## Ingredients
* Yogurt
* Garlic power or raw crushed garlic
* Salt
* Olive oil (and possibly butter mixed in)
* Chili (flakes, whole, powder)

## Steps
1. Mix yogurt with garlic and salt
2. Cook chili in oil (careful not to burn it)
3. Serve yogurt with oil on top

## Variations
* Roast eggplant on stove and serve the eggplant insides under the yogurt
