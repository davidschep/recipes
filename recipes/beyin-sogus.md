# Beyin söğüş (brain)
Boiled brain

## Ingredients
* 1 lamb brain
* Bay leaves
* Olive oil
* Parsley
* 2 tomatoes
* Sumak and oregano

## Steps
1. Clean brains from blood and boil for 20 minutes in salted water (optionally with bay leaves and pepper)
2. Cool down, slice, and serve on plate
3. Dress with olive oil, spices, parsley, and sliced tomatoes
