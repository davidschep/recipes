# Yanak (Beef Cheek)
Slow cooked beef cheek over eggplant bechamel

## Ingredients
* Beef cheek
* Salt, pepper
* Beef stock
* Onion (chopped)
* Carrot (chopped)
* Red wine
* 4 egg plants
* Whole milk
* Flour
* Butter
* Cheese

## Steps
1. Toss the beef cheek in salt and pepper and brown in olive oil on all sides
2. Stew the beef cheeks for around 3 hours in red wine, beef stock, and onion
3. Roast the eggplants on open fire until insides are soft
4. Chop insides
5. Make bechamel, add chopped eggplants and grated cheese
6. Serve soft beef cheek on top of bechamel, with some beef cheek sauce reduced poured over
