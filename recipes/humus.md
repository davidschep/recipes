# Hummus
For a quick version, mix store bought with extra tahini and lemon juice.

## Ingredients
* Dried chickpeas soaked for 8 hours
* Onion and garlic cloves
* Tahini
* Lemon juice
* Salt

## Steps
1. Cook the soaked chickpeas until very soft in water with garlic clove and onion (optionally use pressure cooker)
2. Blend in food processor
3. Add tahini, lemon juice, salt and blend for 5 minutes
4. Serve with more olive oil, pepper powder, parsley

## Variations:
* Add hot pastirma with oil on top
