# Tyrokafteri (white cheese with red peppers)

## Ingredients
* 200 g white cheese
* 3 big red peppers
* 3 cloves of garlic
* Olive oil
* Oregano
* Chili flakes
* Black pepper

## Steps
1. Cook red peppers and garlic in olive oil
2. Blend in food processor
3. Add cheese and spice and blend some more
