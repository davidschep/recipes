# Baba ganuş
Roasted eggplant dish

## Ingredients
* 2 or 3 eggplants
* Tahini
* Lemon juice
* Garnish: olive oil, fresh chili pepper

## Steps
1. Roast eggplants on stove, and optionally finish in the oven
2. Mix chopped eggplant insides with tahini and lemon juice
3. Garnish

## Alternatives
* Muttebel: baba ganuş with yogurt mixed in
