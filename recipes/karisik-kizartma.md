# Karışık Kızartma (fried eggplant with tomato)
Fried eggplant with tomato sauce

## Ingredients
* 1 eggplant
* 2 green peppers
* 2 medium potatoes
* 1 big tomato
* 2 cloves garlic
* Olive oil
* Salt

## Steps
1. Fry eggplant, potatoes, and peppers in olive oil
2. Cook tomato and garlic in separate pan with oil
3. Serve eggplant with tomato sauce on top
