# Dil söğüş (calf tongue)
Boiled tongue

## Ingredients:
* ~1.5 kg veal/beef tongue
* Bay leave, pepper corns, salt
* Olive oil
* Sumak and oregano

## Steps
1. Boil the tongue for 2 hours in salted water with bay leave, pepper corns
2. Peel while hot
3. Cool down and slice
4. Dress with olive oil, sumak, and oregano
