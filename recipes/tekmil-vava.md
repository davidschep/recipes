# Tekmil fava
White fava bean dish

## Ingredients:
* 2 cups white beans
* Onion
* Green onions
* Dill
* Lemon juice
* Olive oil
* Sugar

## Steps
1. Cook chopped onion in olive oil
2. Add beans, salt, sugar, and water
3. Cook and puree when soft
4. Serve with onion, dill, olive oil, lemon juice sauce on top
