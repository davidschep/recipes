# Lakerda (cured fish)
Cured bonito fish

## Ingredients
* Bonito tuna
* Course salt
* Bay leafes

## Steps
1. Clean the fish very well from blood and spine by soaking in clean water multiple times
2. Pack with salt and bay leafes
3. Marinate for 15 days in the fridge
4. Slice and serve
5. Keep for 30 days
